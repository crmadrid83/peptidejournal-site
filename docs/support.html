Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data synced across multiple devices?

A: No. Your data is securely stored on your device and backed up with your iPhone's iCloud Backup or Finder Backup. If you restore your iPhone from a backup, your data will come back with it.

Q: What happens if I delete the app?

A: Deleting the app removes all local data unless you restore from a device backup. Offloading the app (via iOS Settings) will keep your data safe.
