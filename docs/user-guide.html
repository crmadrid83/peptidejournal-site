PeptideJournal User Guide

Complete guide to tracking your peptide routine safely and effectively

⚠️ Important Medical Disclaimer

PeptideJournal is a personal tracking and logging tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals before making any medical decisions. This app is for personal record-keeping purposes only.

🏠 App Overview

PeptideJournal helps you track your peptide routine across five main areas:

Inventory
Calculator
History
Calendar
Analytics

📦 Inventory Management

Your central hub for managing peptide vials and tracking supply levels.

Adding Peptides

Create single peptides or combo vials with multiple compounds. Set concentrations, volumes, batch numbers, and visual colors.

Smart Tracking

Automatically calculates remaining doses and predicts when you'll run out based on your usage patterns.

Swipe Actions

Refill (green), Archive (blue), Delete (red), or Repeat Last Dose (green) with simple swipe gestures.

🧮 Smart Calculator

Precision dose calculations with visual syringe interface for accurate measurements.

How Calculations Work

Volume (mL) = Intended Dose (mg or mcg) ÷ Concentration (mg/mL) Syringe Units = Volume × Syringe Scale Factor

Dual Input Methods

Enter doses in mg or mcg. App automatically converts and calculates exact volume needed.

Syringe Types

Supports insulin syringes (shows units) and TB syringes (shows mL) with precise markings.

Memory Features

Remembers your last dose, syringe settings, and injection route for each peptide.

📝 History & Dose Logging

Complete chronological record of all your injections with comprehensive filtering and search capabilities.

History Tab Features

Chronological Timeline

View all doses in reverse chronological order. Each entry shows peptide name, dose amount, date/time, and quick effect indicators.

Advanced Filtering

Filter by specific peptides, date ranges, injection routes, or effect types. Quickly find patterns in your dosing history.

Swipe Actions

Edit entries, add effects retroactively, or delete incorrect logs with simple swipe gestures on each history item.

What Gets Recorded

  • Date & Time - Automatic timestamp with precise timing
  • Peptide & Dose - Name, intended dose, and actual volume injected
  • Syringe Details - Type (insulin/TB), scale, and unit display preferences
  • Injection Route - Subcutaneous, intramuscular, or other
  • Side Effects - Predefined categories plus custom "other" entries
  • Benefits - Positive effects with detailed tracking options
  • Notes & Sites - Injection locations and personal observations

📊 Analytics & Insights

Transform your data into actionable insights about your peptide routine.

Core Statistics

Usage Tracking

Active peptides, total injection sessions, and comprehensive dose history.

Effectiveness Score

Algorithm ranks peptides: (Benefits per dose × 2) - Side effects per dose

Consistency Metrics

Dosing streaks, adherence rates, and achievement badges for motivation.

How to Interpret Your Analytics

📊 Pie Charts

Peptide Usage: Shows proportion of total doses per peptide. Larger slices = more frequently used compounds.

Side Effects: Displays which adverse effects occur most often. Monitor red sections for concerning patterns.

Benefits: Highlights positive effects frequency. Green sections show your most effective outcomes.

📈 Trend Charts

Side Effect Bars: Weekly view with color coding - Green (none), Orange (1-2), Red (3+). Upward trends may indicate dosing issues.

Weight Trends: 90-day swipeable chart. Look for correlations between peptide cycles and weight changes.

🏆 Top Performer

Algorithm ranks peptides by effectiveness score. Higher scores indicate better benefit-to-side-effect ratios. Requires minimum 3 doses for accuracy.

⚡ Consistency Badges

Perfect Week (7 days): Yellow badge with crown icon

Consistency Master (14 days): Blue badge with target icon

Early Bird (30 days): Green badge with sun icon

🔒 Privacy & Data

Your health data stays secure and private with local storage and optional integrations.

Data Storage & Security

  • Local Database - All data stored on your device using SwiftData with SQLite backend
  • iCloud Backup Support - PeptideJournal supports iCloud Backup, which securely includes your data when you back up your device. It does not sync data across multiple devices. To move data, use iCloud backup restore or CSV export/import.
  • HealthKit Integration - Optional weight sync (requires explicit permission)
  • CSV Export/Import - Full data portability for backup, analysis, or device migration
  • Data Integrity - Automatic validation and recovery mechanisms protect against corruption

Backup & Data Management

Export Options

Export complete dose history, weight data, or peptide inventory as CSV files for external analysis or backup.

Import Capabilities

Import previous data from CSV files when switching devices or restoring from backup.

Selective Deletion

Clear weight data independently or delete individual dose entries via swipe actions in History tab.

Device Backups

Your data is automatically included in your iPhone's encrypted iCloud Backup or Finder Backup. Export CSV files for additional security or device migration.
