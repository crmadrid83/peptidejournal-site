Privacy Policy for PeptideJournal

Last updated: August 31, 2025

Key Point: PeptideJournal stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

PeptideJournal is designed with privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does NOT collect personal information - No names, emails, or contact details
  • Does NOT transmit app data - All app information stays on your device
  • Uses privacy-friendly, cookie-free website analytics on peptidejournal.net only, to understand aggregate traffic (page views, referrers, device types, countries). No personal data is collected or stored, and no cross-site tracking occurs.
  • Does NOT require accounts - No sign-up or login required

2. Data Storage

All data you enter into PeptideJournal is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Peptide inventory information
  • Dose calculations and history
  • Calendar entries and reminders
  • App preferences and settings

3. Data Backup

PeptideJournal data is automatically backed up through Apple's standard iOS backup system:

  • iCloud Backup - App data is included in your device's iCloud backup (if enabled in iOS Settings)
  • Local iTunes/Finder Backup - Data is included when you backup your device to your computer
  • CSV Export - Manual export functionality for additional data portability
  • Privacy Protected - All backups remain within Apple's ecosystem and are never transmitted to our servers

4. HealthKit & Health Data

PeptideJournal may optionally integrate with Apple's HealthKit to:

  • Read weight data - To auto-populate weight fields in dose calculations
  • Write weight data - To save weight entries you make in the app

Important HealthKit Privacy Protections:

  • We obtain your explicit consent before accessing any health data
  • Health data is never used for advertising, marketing, or sold to third parties
  • HealthKit data is only used to provide health and fitness features within the app
  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings
  • All health data remains on your device and in your personal iCloud Health data (if enabled)

5. Third-Party Services & Analytics

The iOS app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Our public website (peptidejournal.net) uses Cloudflare Web Analytics (cookie-free, privacy-first) to measure aggregate usage with a 30-day retention period. It does not collect personally identifiable information (PII), does not set cookies, and does not track users across sites.

6. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS device security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Apple's secure data storage frameworks
  • Local encryption when backed up to iCloud

7. Medical Disclaimer

PeptideJournal is intended for research tracking purposes only. It is not a medical device and should not be used for medical diagnosis or treatment decisions. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice.

8. California Privacy Rights (CPRA/CCPA)

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information as defined by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CPRA). Since no personal data is collected, California residents have no personal information to request, delete, or opt-out from.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
